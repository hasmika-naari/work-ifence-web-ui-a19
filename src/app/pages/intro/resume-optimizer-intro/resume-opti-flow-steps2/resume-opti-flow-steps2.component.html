Now let’s talk about why it’s a problem.​

Applicants often face challenges and frustrations when navigating the AI-driven resume screening process.

Lack of Transparency

Applicants find AI screening processes vague, making it difficult to tailor their resumes due to unclear evaluation criteria and prioritized qualifications.

Keyword Optimization Pressure

Applicants may resort to keyword stuffing to pass automated screenings, resulting in formulaic resumes that lack a genuine reflection of their skills and experiences.

Inability to Show Soft Skills

Soft skills, crucial for many roles, are hard to convey in AI screenings, making it difficult for applicants to highlight qualities like adaptability and leadership.
