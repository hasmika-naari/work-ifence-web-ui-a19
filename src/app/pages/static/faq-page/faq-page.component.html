Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Work iFence?

A: Work iFence is an online platform that offers services to manage resumes and job applications. It also provides the latest job openings, learning resources, and training materials

Q: What kinds of training resources does Work iFence offer?

A:Work iFence provides a list of online and in-person 1:1 or group training sessions from experts across the globe. It also offers highly rated training videos from sources such as YouTube, Udemy, and more.

Q: Where does Work iFence Stores Created Resume?

A: Work iFence stores generated resumes in S3 storage in PDF format and stores the actual data in JSON format in the Work iFence database as a TextBlob.

Q: What is Work iFence AI Support Feature?

A: Work iFence is integrated with ChatGPT open API, and provides you on demand support for the selected sections of Resume based on your workIfence Subscription level.

Q: Can I post my own deals or deals I find on Work iFence?

A: Unfortunately, this is not a feature we provide. Although we hope to incorporate this into our site soon enough!

Q: Can I save deals for later on Work iFence?

A: The wishlist feature is one that is under the works right now and will be released in our very next launch.
